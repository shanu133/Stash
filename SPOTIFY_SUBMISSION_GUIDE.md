## Quick Reference: Adding Users to Your App

Since Stash is in **Spotify Development Mode**, you need to manually add users before they can log in.

### Steps to Add Users:

1. **Go to Spotify Developer Dashboard**
   - Visit: https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard
   - Log in with your Spotify account

2. **Select Your Stash App**
   - Click on your "Stash" application

3. **Navigate to User Management**
   - Click **Settings** in the left sidebar
   - Scroll to **User Management** section

4. **Add Users**
   - Click **Add User**
   - Enter the user's **Spotify email address**
   - Click **Add**
   - Repeat for up to 25 users

5. **Notify Users**
   - Tell users they can now log in at: https://stashyourmusic.vercel.app
   - They must use the same Spotify account associated with the email you added

## When to Apply for Extended Quota Mode

**Don't apply yet if:**
- You have fewer than 250,000 monthly active users
- You don't have a registered business entity
- You're still in beta/testing phase

**Apply when:**
- You've reached 250,000+ MAU
- You have an LLC, Inc., or similar business entity
- You have a company email address
- You have privacy policy and terms of service
- You have user metrics and analytics ready
